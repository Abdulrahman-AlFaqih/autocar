Who We Are

AlRaebi Auto Car is a leading company established with the aim of creating a qualitative leap in the automotive sector in the Middle East, by providing integrated solutions in the supply, sale, and maintenance of electric vehicles.

Our Mission

To provide the best electric vehicles with the latest global specifications, supported by specialized maintenance services, to meet our customers' aspirations and achieve the highest levels of satisfaction and trust.

Our Vision

To be the first and leading choice in the electric vehicle market, and to actively contribute to the shift towards green mobility in the region.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Delivering cutting-edge technologies.
  • Quality: Excellence in performance & service.
  • Transparency: Honest & clear customer interactions.
  • Sustainability: Promoting a cleaner environment.
  • Commitment: Professional & responsible service.
